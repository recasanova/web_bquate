Biografía

Carlos Vives was born on 7 August 1961 in Santa Marta, Magdalena, Colombia, where he spent his first 12 years of life. At that age, he and his family moved to Bogotá in search of a better life. They lived with Diego Martínez and his family. In Bogota, he acquired a taste for rock, getting involved in the local music scene, and started playing in bars and cafés around the city.

In 1989, he was offered an acting job in Puerto Rico, and upon moving, he took a break in his music career. He is remembered for his leading roles in the soaps La Otra and Aventurera. He married Herlinda Gómez, his second wife (they have since divorced). Vives would spend his time between Colombia, Miami and the city of Mayagüez, Herlinda's hometown, during his marriage to her.

DIIV presenta su nuevo single

'Under the Sun' es el reciente estreno de DIIV. La banda que nos visitó en el Movistar Primavera Fauna prepara la salida de su segundo álbum, "Is the Is Are". En su promoción, se han oficializado otros tres avances: 'Dopamine', 'Bent (Roi's Song)' y 'Mire (Grant's Song)', cortes que buscan igualar los elogios que alcanzó su debut, "Oshin" (2012).

Se ha sumado un nombre más al cartel del Festival en Órbita. El encuentro, del que se han confirmado las presencias de The Sonics, Os Mutantes, Dead Meadow, A Place to Bury Strangers, Los Jaivas y The Dandy Warhols, entre otros, se desarrollará en Espacio Broadway.
